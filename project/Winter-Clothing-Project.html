We are pleased to announce the completion of our second project (Health Awareness), which targeted several primary and secondary schools including:

Primary Schools

  • Musa
  • Education
  • Sudanese-Saudi Friendship
  • Sudanese-Dutch Friendship
  • Rayyan
  • Naseeba

Secondary Schools

  • Mahara
  • Al-Zahraa
  • Umm Al-Mu'minin

It was one of the most beautiful projects we have implemented, and we extend our sincere thanks to all the teachers (male and female) and to everyone who contributed and participated in making this project a success.

Now we face a new challenge and a new project, hoping to achieve excellence and uniqueness in it. Together, we can overcome difficulties. Let us join hands to make the Winter Clothing Project a success.

Project Goals

Provide Warm Clothing

Distribute winter clothing to students in need to protect them from cold weather conditions.

Community Support

Strengthen community bonds by encouraging participation and collective responsibility.

Sustainable Impact

Create lasting impact by ensuring students can attend school comfortably during winter months.

How You Can Help

Financial Donations

Your monetary contributions help us purchase quality winter clothing in bulk at discounted prices.

Clothing Donations

New or gently used winter clothing items (coats, sweaters, gloves, scarves, etc.) are greatly appreciated.

Volunteer

Join our team to help with collection, sorting, distribution, and awareness activities.

Spread the Word

Share our campaign with your network to help us reach more potential supporters.
