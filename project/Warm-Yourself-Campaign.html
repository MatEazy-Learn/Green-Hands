About the Campaign

The "Warm Yourself" campaign is a joint initiative by the Green Hands and Mutakafulon Associations to provide winter clothing to vulnerable groups in Nyala and surrounding areas.

Have you ever seen winter at night? How can you be so cozy under a blanket, feel warm, sleep soundly, and wish the night would last longer? There are some people who wish the night would never come because they don't have blankets or clothes to protect them from the cold.

If your sweater is too small for you, or if you bought a new one and are no longer wearing it, call us and we'll come to collect it. Your donation can bring warmth to someone in need.

Campaign Details

Launch Date

The campaign officially started on Saturday, December 21, 2019, and continues every winter season.

Location

We distribute winter clothing to vulnerable groups in various neighborhoods of Nyala.

Partnership

A joint effort between Green Hands Association and Mutakafulon Association.
