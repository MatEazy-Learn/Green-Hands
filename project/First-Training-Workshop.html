First Training Workshop

Capacity Development & Skills Enhancement Project

#Green_Hands_All_Love

Praise be to God

The Green Hands Association has successfully concluded its first training workshop under the Capacity Development and Skills Enhancement Project. This milestone marks the beginning of our journey to empower communities with sustainable skills and knowledge.

Workshop Highlights

  • Interactive sessions on sustainable practices
  • Hands-on training with expert facilitators
  • Networking opportunities with like-minded individuals
  • Practical skill-building exercises
  • Certification for all participants

Workshop Moments

Interactive learning session with participants

Engaging group discussions and activities

Practical demonstration of sustainable techniques

Team building exercises during the workshop

Certificate distribution to proud participants

Memorable group photo to conclude the workshop
