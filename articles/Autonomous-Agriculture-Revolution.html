Our Robotic Solutions

Cutting-edge autonomous systems designed for maximum efficiency and sustainability

Autonomous Plowing System

Key Components

  • AI Path Planning
  • Soil Sensors
  • Safety System

Tools

  • GPS-RTK + NVIDIA Jetson
  • Veris pH/EC sensors
  • LiDAR + 360° cameras

Self-driving tractors like John Deere 8R (AutoPath™) or Kubota X-tractor generate fuel-efficient plowing routes with sub-inch accuracy while automatically adjusting to soil conditions.

Robotic Irrigation System

Key Components

  • Rainwater Harvesting
  • Autonomous Delivery
  • Precision Nozzles

Tools

  • Polyethylene tanks
  • FarmBot Genesis
  • Variable-rate sprayers

Solar-powered drones (Aerialtronics Altura Zenith) or ground rovers (TerraSentia) deliver precise irrigation based on real-time soil moisture data, reducing water waste by 30-50%.

Rainwater Harvesting System

Our underground rainwater storage tanks (50,000L capacity per hectare) with IoT-enabled ultrasonic sensors ensure optimal water management. The system collects, filters, and stores rainwater for autonomous irrigation.

  • Automated water level and purity monitoring
  • Hybrid backup with treated wastewater
  • 40% reduction in water usage compared to traditional irrigation

Risk Management

Proactive strategies to ensure project success and continuity

Risk Mitigation Strategy
Connectivity Loss Offline AI modes (e.g., Raven Autonomy's edge computing)
Hardware Failure On-site spare parts + modular robot designs
Water Shortage Hybrid sources: Rainwater + treated wastewater
Regulatory Barriers Collaborate with local agencies (e.g., UN-FAO) for compliance frameworks

Implementation Tip

Start with retrofitting existing tractors with autonomy kits (e.g., Bear Flag Robotics) before investing in new robots. This phased approach reduces initial costs while demonstrating the benefits of automation.
