أزماتنا

Our Reality Demands Action

Sudan faces overlapping emergencies of war toxins, climate disasters, and broken systems—but our people have the power to heal.

War's Toxic Legacy

Chemical weapons banned by the world have seeped into our earth and water, poisoning fields and wells.

  • Invisible toxins in soil and water sources
  • Rising birth defects and cancers in affected areas
  • Farmlands abandoned due to contamination

Climate's Cruel Cycle

Floods drown our crops, then droughts starve what remains—breaking our ancient balance with nature.

  • Erratic rains destroying harvests
  • Longer droughts drying up wells
  • Desertification swallowing villages

Broken Systems, Unbroken People

While weapons flow in, medicine and tools do not—but we are building our own solutions.

  • Blocked aid and medical supplies
  • Corrupt distribution systems
  • Community networks bypassing barriers

حلولنا

Our Plan: By Sudan, For Sudan

Combining traditional wisdom with proven science to heal our land and people.

Threat Green Hands' Response Your Role
Chemical Contamination Train 100 local "Earth Scouts" to test water/soil; plant toxin-cleaning gardens with sunflowers and ferns. Share knowledge of affected areas; guard community labs.
Floods & Droughts Restore 50+ hafirs (ancient water basins); distribute 10,000+ native tree saplings (acacia, baobab) to anchor soil. Lead land restoration on your family's plot.
War Trauma Mobile healing circles with elders + psychologists; grants for women's farming co-ops. Host gatherings; teach traditional healing herbs.

Why This Works

  • No Foreign Experts

    Tools like portable water testers (used in Syria) and flood-resistant farming (modeled on Bangladesh) are adapted by you, for you.

  • Justice + Ecology

    We document toxin damage to demand global accountability while acting now—like Vietnam's Phytoremediation Project that cleaned Agent Orange from soil.

  • Sacred Duty

    As the Quran teaches: "Do not corrupt the earth after it has been set right" (Surah Al-A'raf 7:56). This is our covenant.
