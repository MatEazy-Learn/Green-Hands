Green Hands Charity Association

Planting hope and extending helping hands to communities affected by climate change in South Darfur

Our Story

The Green Hands Charity Association is a newly established nonprofit organization based in Nyala, South Darfur, founded by a dedicated group of young Sudanese committed to environmental and humanitarian action.

Born out of a shared commitment to protect lives and preserve our natural environment, our organization responds to the growing environmental and humanitarian challenges—especially climate change and recurrent flooding—through community-driven, sustainable action.

What began as a small group of passionate youth has grown into a movement of change-makers working hand in hand with local communities to protect lives, restore livelihoods, and create a greener, safer future for all.

Our Mission & Vision

Building resilience through community action and environmental stewardship

Our Mission

To support communities affected by climate change and flooding through community outreach, environmental education, and emergency response initiatives. We aim to build resilience, raise awareness, and promote sustainable solutions.

Our Vision

A future where communities in South Darfur are resilient to climate shocks, where youth are empowered as environmental stewards, and where sustainable practices protect both people and nature.

Our Core Goals

Focused initiatives to address urgent environmental and humanitarian needs

Rapid Disaster Response

Provide urgent relief and support to families affected by floods and environmental disasters in South Darfur.

Environmental Awareness

Organize awareness campaigns to educate local communities about climate change dangers and adaptation methods.

Community Engagement

Encourage youth and citizens to participate in local environmental and developmental initiatives.

Infrastructure Rehabilitation

Assist in repairing damage caused by floods, including homes, schools, and healthcare centers.

Afforestation & Protection

Launch tree-planting and neighborhood-cleaning campaigns to combat desertification and flooding.

Sustainable Agriculture

Promote climate-smart farming techniques to improve food security in vulnerable communities.

Our Future Plans

Strategic initiatives to expand our impact and reach

Emergency Response Center

Establish a fully equipped emergency response center with essential supplies for use during disasters, ensuring rapid assistance to affected communities.

Volunteer Training

Train local volunteer teams in first aid, disaster management, and environmental conservation to build community capacity for response.

Strategic Partnerships

Build partnerships with local and international organizations to strengthen our efforts with additional resources and technical expertise.

Flood-Risk Mapping

Create an interactive flood-risk map in collaboration with relevant authorities to help monitor and prevent future disasters.

Education Programs

Launch environmental education programs in schools and universities to promote volunteerism and sustainability among youth.

Afforestation Projects

Implement seasonal afforestation projects in flood-prone and environmentally vulnerable areas to combat desertification.

Join Our Movement

At Green Hands, we believe that youth are the driving force of change. Through unity, dedication, and action, we aim to make a meaningful impact—helping both people and nature thrive.
